About Me

My name is Megan Hansen. I am a Literature, Media and Communications major at Georgia Tech concentrating in communciations and social justice. Not only am I a dedicated student, but I am also a part of the Georgia Tech Varsity Swim Team. The swim team has taught me time management, dedication, and has encouraged my competitive spirit. I love learning new things and exploring my interests including law, politics, and sports.

My Mission

Athletics has always been how I identify myself, but now that I am graduating, I want to take my competitive nature into the corporate world. Recruiting, marketing, sales, and advertising are what I am most interested in. With the rise of social media advertising, it is important to be able to communicate your message as quickly and effectively as possible.

My goal is to take what I have learned in school and athletics to sell your product to the world. My competitive background will help me be successful in this field. I have put together a collection of written works and images on this site to showcase my talent and passions.
