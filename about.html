Georgia Tech Swimming

Swimming is a huge part of my life. Sports photos are a new found passion of mine. Swimming for Georgia Tech has gien me many opportunites, one of them being recruiting. As a Varsity athlete, I am expected to reach out and make the potential future members of our team feel welcomed and wanted. Not only that, but ensuring that the people being recruited are a good fit for us and we are a good fit for them.

Social media advertising is a big part of this. Using images of the team to show our culture on platforms like Instagram and Twitter is very important and a skill I feel I can use in a future job.
